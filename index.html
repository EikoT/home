So what about me?

Video games has always been my passion and it has been my childhood dream to work in the video game industry. My journey began at BCIT where I studied programming for a couple years… only to find out it wasn’t for me.

I found myself at a loss but kept myself busy with the BCIT Game Development club where we started planning a game jam for students. In 2016 we recruited game development clubs from UBC and SFU to help organize the event. By 2017 had our very first BC Game Jam and we’ve been holding it annually ever since!

After the first BC Game Jam, one of the organizers, George Padua, recruited me to join his new company: Raccoopack Studios. He was impressed with how I was managing our social media and Discord channels during BC Game Jam and he offered me to try community management over at his studio… and that’s where I took my first step!
